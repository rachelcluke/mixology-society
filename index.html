About Us

We are a fun, laid back society aimed at anyone who wants to make friends, gain new experiences and just have a good time! Our socials take place every Tuesday and we would love for you to join us.

Experiences

Cocktail society for us, is more of an excuse to host some super fun events and form some new friendships. We run some really fun events which include: Pizza Nights, Cocktail Masterclasses, a Masquerade murder mystery, Christmas cocktail party and more....

Inclusivity Matters

We believe that everyone should have a place at the cocktail table. Whether you're abstaining from alcohol or simply prefer the flavours of a mocktail, we've got you covered. We have even partnered with ‘The sober bartender’ this year, a completely non-alcoholic online store.

Member Perks

This year, we are really stepping up our discount game. Being a member means you can get exclusive discounts and freebies thanks to our sponsors (Bar Halo and Local Booze)! This includes 2-4-1 drinks at Bar Halo, subsidized Mixology Masterclasses and much more!
